Our Story & Journey

A passion for flavor, hospitality & unforgettable memories

Our Story

Salt Restaurant was born from a simple belief — great food brings people together. We pioneered the live-grill-at-your-table concept, letting guests season and grill their own starters while enjoying an unlimited buffet of Indian and global cuisines.

From a single outlet to multiple branches, our fixed-price all-you-can-eat model offers consistently delicious food at value-oriented prices in a warm, casual dining environment.

Every dish is crafted with fresh spices, seasonal ingredients, and the love that makes Salt Restaurant a household name among food lovers.

Why Salt?

The name "Salt" represents the essential ingredient that elevates every dish — just as we aim to elevate every dining experience. Our core values prioritize being customer-focused and employee-focused, creating a superior work environment and excellent service.

Whether it's a birthday celebration, corporate lunch, or a casual family dinner — Salt Restaurant is where memories are made over great food.
